🕹️ What is Crossy Road?
Crossy Road is a modern take on the classic frogger-style game. Developed by Hipster Whale, it challenges players to guide their character across busy roads, swift rivers, and fast-moving train tracks without getting squashed, drowned, or snatched by an eagle! It’s widely known as the ultimate chicken-crossing game, but there's so much more to explore.
